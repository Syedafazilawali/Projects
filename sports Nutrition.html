Oatmeal Benefits


Oatmeal is a type of coarse flour made of hulled oat grains that have either been milled or steel-cut, or else it refers to rolled oats. Ground oats are also called "white oats". Steel-cut oats are known as "coarse oatmeal", "Irish oatmeal" or "pinhead oats".
